Can you explain Banker's Algorithm from Operating Systems?
✨

The Banker's Algorithm is a resource allocation and deadlock avoidance algorithm.

It tests for safety by simulating the allocation for predetermined maximum possible amounts of all resources, and then makes a "s-state" check to test for possible activities, before deciding whether allocation should be allowed to continue.
